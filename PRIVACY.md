# Local Lines Privacy Policy

Local Lines does not collect, sell, or share personal data.

The optional live-map provider requests device location to render nearby roads.
Location is processed on the watch. Only the vector-tile coordinates required
to draw the map are requested from OpenFreeMap. Local Lines stores the last
successful coordinate, update time, and rendered map on the watch so it can
work offline; it does not retain location history.

The applications contain no account system, advertising, analytics, or paid
service. Users can revoke location access or clear stored data through Wear OS
settings at any time.

Map tiles are provided by OpenFreeMap using OpenStreetMap and OpenMapTiles data.
Their respective privacy policies and terms apply to requests made to that
service.

Last updated: 12 July 2026.
